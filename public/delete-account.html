Delete Your Account

Voice of the People  ·  Permanent account deletion

⚠️ Account deletion is permanent. Your data cannot be recovered after the 90-day retention window expires.

Delete from within the app

1
Open the Voice of the People app and sign in.
2
Tap the Profile tab at the bottom of the screen.
3
Scroll down and tap Settings > Account.
4
Tap Delete Account and confirm when prompted.

Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, email us from the address associated with your account:

Email deletion request

We will process your request within 7 business days and confirm by email.

What gets deleted

  • Your name, email address, and profile information
  • Your district assignments and voter verification status
  • Your questions, responses, and upvotes
  • Your account credentials and session data

Data is permanently purged after a 90-day retention window required for fraud prevention and legal compliance. Anonymized, aggregate analytics data (which cannot identify you) may be retained.
