Privacy Policy

Voice of the People  ·  Effective Date: June 28, 2026  ·  Version 1.0

1. Introduction

Voice of the People ("we," "us," or "our") is a civic engagement platform that connects Wisconsin voters with political candidates. We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we collect, use, and share it.

By creating an account or using the Voice of the People mobile application (the "App"), you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Full Name: Used to create your voter profile and verify eligibility.
  • Email Address: Used for account authentication, notifications, and communications.
  • Residential Address (Hashed): We hash your address using a one-way cryptographic process. We do not store your street address in plain text.
  • Voting District: Derived from your address hash via the Google Civic Information API.
  • Government-Issued Identity Data (via Persona): To verify you are a real Wisconsin resident aged 18+. We receive only a pass/fail result — we do not store copies of your ID documents.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device model, OS version, and unique device identifiers
  • App usage data (pages viewed, features used, time spent)
  • Log data: IP address, timestamps, crash reports
  • Push notification tokens

2.3 Information From Third Parties

  • Google Civic Information API: District lookup from your hashed address.
  • Persona: Identity verification result and fraud-risk signal.

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Platform Operation

  • Authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • Display your verified voter status to candidates in your districts.
  • Route your questions to the candidates who represent your districts.
  • Enforce eligibility requirements (Wisconsin resident, 18+).

3.2 Political Advertising

Voice of the People displays targeted political advertisements. Ads are targeted by voting district only. We do not share your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information with Political Advertisers. Every political advertisement is clearly labeled "PAID POLITICAL ADVERTISEMENT" and includes the sponsor's name and FEC registration number where applicable.

You may opt out of political advertising at any time in Settings > Privacy > Political Advertising.

4. How We Share Your Information

4.1 With Candidates

Candidates in your districts can see your first name and last initial, public questions you've submitted, and your verified district memberships. They cannot see your email address or full address.

4.2 With Political Advertisers

We share only aggregate, anonymized data — district-level engagement statistics and ad performance metrics. We never share personally identifiable information with advertisers.

4.3 With Service Providers

  • Supabase: Cloud database and authentication
  • Stripe: Payment processing (PCI-DSS compliant)
  • Persona: Identity verification
  • Upstash: Caching and rate limiting
  • Google Civic Information API: District lookup

4.4 No Sale of Personal Data

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Retained while your account is active.
  • Deleted accounts: Retained for 90 days for fraud prevention, then permanently deleted.
  • Anonymized data: May be retained indefinitely for analytics.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: Request a copy of your data at privacy@voiceofthepeople.com.
  • Correction: Update your info in Settings > Account.
  • Deletion: Delete your account in Settings > Account > Delete Account.
  • Opt out of political ads: Settings > Privacy > Political Advertising.
  • California/state residents: Contact us to exercise CCPA/CPRA rights.

7. Security

We use TLS 1.2+ encryption in transit, AES-256 encryption at rest, row-level security (RLS) via Supabase, identity verification via Persona, and rate limiting via Upstash. In the event of a data breach, we will notify you within 72 hours as required by law.

8. Children's Privacy

Voice of the People is not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. Contact us at privacy@voiceofthepeople.com to report an underage account.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via in-app notice. Continued use of the App after the effective date constitutes acceptance.
